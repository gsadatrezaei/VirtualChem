VirtualChem SSD

Link to VirtualChem website: https://you.stonybrook.edu/323virtualchem/
Link to the git repository: https://github.com/gsadatrezaei/VirtualChem

To play the VirtualChem educational game:
press [A] to create a Carbon atom(black), press [S] to create a Hydrogen atom(white), press[D] to create an Oxygen atom(Red),
and press [Q] to create a bond.
To delete an atom select it and press the SPACE key.
To rotate bond, while pressing the left mouse button, press the UP-Arrow or DOWN-Arrow key.
